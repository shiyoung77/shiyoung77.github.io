Shiyang Lu (卢施阳)

I'm a final-year Ph.D. candidate in the Computer Science Department at Rutgers University. I'm primarily advised by Prof. Kostas Bekris in the CS Robotics lab. I also work closely with Prof. Abdeslam Boularias. My research interest lies in 3D computer vision (segmentation, pose estimation, 3D reconstruction, etc.) and its applications in robotics, such as robot manipulation.

Previously, I earned a M.S. degree in Robotics from the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor, where I worked in the Lab for Progress directed by Prof. Chad Jenkins. I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Naval Architecture and Ocean Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

I will join Meta Reality Lab as a Research Scientist in July 2024 :)
